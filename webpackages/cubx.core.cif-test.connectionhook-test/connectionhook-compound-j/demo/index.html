Demo page for compound component connectionhook-compound-j

This compound component demostrate the global hookFunction between two member (connectionhook-compound-f and connectionhook-compound-g) The connection between the members is without hookFunction and the members are compound components.

  • connectionhook-compound-f - component with internal connections without hookFunctions.
  • connectionhook-compound-g - component with internal connections with hookFunctions.

Connected connectionhook-compound-e (component with hook function) with connectionhook-compound-h (component without connenction hook) without connection hook.
